Drummond has spent the last decade running marketing and growth for technology companies, latterly as a fractional CMO. He is a graduate of the University of Oxford's AI programme, teaches on AI at Saïd Business School, and organises Oxford's Practical AI meetup.

He started the bootcamp for a straightforward reason: plenty of senior people in Oxford had done the reading, taken the executive course, and still had not built anything. The gap was never understanding. It was a day, a room, and someone to unstick you when the tool misbehaves.
